Find symptoms, causes, and solutions to protect your crops.

1. Apple Scab

Symptoms: Dark spots on leaves and fruit.

Cause: Fungus Venturia inaequalis.

Solution: Remove fallen leaves and spray fungicide.

2. Apple Black Rot

Symptoms: Circular black spots on fruit and branches.

Cause: Fungus Botryosphaeria obtusa.

Solution: Prune affected areas and apply fungicide.

3. Apple Cedar Rust

Symptoms: Orange spots on leaves, causing leaf drop.

Cause: Fungus from junipers.

Solution: Remove nearby junipers and spray fungicide.

4. Cherry Powdery Mildew

Symptoms: White powder on leaves and fruits.

Cause: Fungus Podosphaera clandestina.

Solution: Improve airflow and use sulfur-based fungicides.

5. Corn Gray Leaf Spot

Symptoms: Gray lesions on leaves.

Cause: Fungus Cercospora zeae-maydis.

Solution: Crop rotation and fungicide application.

6. Grape Black Rot

Symptoms: Black spots on leaves and fruit.

Cause: Fungus Guignardia bidwellii.

Solution: Remove infected fruit and use fungicides.

7. Peach Bacterial Spot

Symptoms: Water-soaked spots on fruit.

Cause: Bacteria Xanthomonas campestris.

Solution: Use copper sprays and avoid overhead watering.

8. Potato Early Blight

Symptoms: Brown spots with rings on leaves.

Cause: Fungus Alternaria solani.

Solution: Rotate crops and apply fungicide.

9. Potato Late Blight

Symptoms: Water-soaked lesions on leaves.

Cause: Fungus Phytophthora infestans.

Solution: Remove infected plants and spray fungicides.

10. Tomato Leaf Mold

Symptoms: Yellow spots on top, mold underneath leaves.

Cause: Fungus Passalora fulva.

Solution: Increase airflow and use fungicides.

11. Tomato Target Spot

Symptoms: Brown spots with rings on fruit and leaves.

Cause: Fungus Corynespora cassiicola.

Solution: Remove infected parts and use copper sprays.

12. Tomato Mosaic Virus

Symptoms: Mottled leaves and poor fruit quality.

Cause: Virus spread by contaminated tools.

Solution: Use clean tools and destroy affected plants.

13. Tomato Yellow Leaf Curl Virus

Symptoms: Yellowing and curling of leaves.

Cause: Virus spread by whiteflies.

Solution: Control whiteflies and plant resistant varieties.

14. Strawberry Leaf Scorch

Symptoms: Purple blotches on leaves.

Cause: Fungus.

Solution: Remove affected leaves and apply fungicides.

15. Healthy Plant

Tip: Check crops regularly, maintain soil health, and use proper irrigation techniques.
